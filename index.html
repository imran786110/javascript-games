Game 1

Guess my Number

In this game you have to guess a number between 1 to 20, if it exactly matches my guess, then you will win and the high score can be stored below.

Use the input field below to write your number and click on check button. It will check if the number matches or not.

There you can also see the information about your number is greater than or smaller than my number, that gives a good hint to guess for the next time.

Game 2

Roll it

Here you need two players to play the game. Currently it is no available to play on multiple screens.
Enter your names on the first screen.

The game starts with player 1, you can roll the diceuntil you see the number 1.

Your score will be added to the previous sum. when you get 1 the player will be switched. The player who scores 100 earlier is the winner.
